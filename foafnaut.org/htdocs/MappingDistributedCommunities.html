This is a huge topic that I am not sure we should even attempt to tackle. However, the following are clearly design issues:
  • Enclosing groups
  • Distance of individual reach through community network should not be beyond authorised groups.
  • Trusting and verifying FOAF data
  • Codes of conduct which can be designed into the interface
