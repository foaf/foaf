Modesty...

So I really wanted to make a page about modesty. RDFWeb could easily sound rather grand and pretentious. It is certainly ambitious. But it is basically about building something fun based on some old old old ideas, and seeing if they can be tweaked so that they scale to the Web.

I wanted to make RDFWeb be all about finding a balance between modesty, sarcasm and wide-eyed idealism. But it's probably about hacking and people and networks and stuff.

I'll shut up now.
