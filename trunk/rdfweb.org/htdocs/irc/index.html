RDFWeb IRC Developer Chat

Chat logs: #foaf IRC logs (latest)

The publically logged #foaf channel on irc.freenode.net is devoted to FOAF, Co-depiction, RDFWeb and related topics. You're very welcome to stop by if you have any questions, ideas or concerns about FOAF, or if you just want to hang out and lurk. There is sometimes a copy of foafbot running in the channel, see the foafbot site for more details.

Many #foaf and RDF folk can also be found in the #rdfig channel nearby. See also the RDFIG blog and logs
