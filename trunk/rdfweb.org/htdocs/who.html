People

RDFWeb is collaborative project created by Dan Brickley and Libby Miller.

Lots of other people have contributed, either by participating in rdfweb-dev or real life discussions, by creating software tools (RDF, XML and other stuff), or by creating content to fill RDFWeb with interesting information.
