FOAFCorp: Corporate Friends of Friends

What is this?

RDF is a great data format for describing interconnected entities. With FOAF, we have a simple fun vocabulary for describing social networks, people, organisations etc. The FOAFCorp experiment extends this to describe in more detail the structure and interconnections of corporate entities. The sample data here draws on the excellent They Rule approach, listing the board membership for three companies.
